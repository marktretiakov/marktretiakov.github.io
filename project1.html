Robotic Arm

I conceptualized and developed a high-precision 3-axis robotic arm using SolidWorks, demonstrating advanced proficiency in CAD modeling and mechanical engineering. The arm was meticulously designed to perform precise movements and object manipulation, making it adaptable to a wide range of applications. Leveraging 3D printing technology, I fabricated each component with exceptional attention to detail, ensuring structural integrity, dimensional accuracy, and smooth assembly.

To bring the arm to life, I implemented an Arduino-based control system, integrating servo motors to achieve accurate and responsive motion. The electrical wiring and programming were carefully executed to enable seamless operation and precise control. This project reflects a comprehensive skill set, merging mechanical design, prototyping, and embedded systems engineering to create a functional and innovative robotic solution.
