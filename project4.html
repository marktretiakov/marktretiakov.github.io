Formula Electric

As a member of the university’s Formula Electric team, I designed critical components for the vehicle’s braking system, including the balance bar clevis and center trunnion. The balance bar clevis is responsible for securing the balance bar within the braking system, ensuring that force is properly transferred between the pedal and the master cylinders. Its precise design is essential for maintaining stability and efficient performance within the system.

The images showcase the balance bar clevis and center trunnion integrated into the overall braking assembly. These components are key to the system's structural integrity and functionality, ensuring that the braking forces are properly distributed. Their seamless integration highlights my ability to design and contribute to complex mechanical systems that require precision and reliability in high-performance environments.
