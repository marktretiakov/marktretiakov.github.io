Jet Turbine Assembly

I designed and assembled a detailed jet turbine model using SolidWorks, showcasing advanced proficiency in CAD modeling and mechanical engineering principles. The project involved creating intricate components and ensuring precise alignment and functionality within the assembly.

This project required meticulous attention to detail and a strong understanding of mechanical design principles. The final assembly was validated through simulations, demonstrating its structural integrity and accurate representation of a real-world jet turbine.
